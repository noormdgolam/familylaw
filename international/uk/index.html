Overview of United Kingdom Family Law

In the UK, the introduction of 'no-fault' divorce in 2022 fundamentally changed the landscape, allowing couples to separate without apportioning blame. Courts prioritize the welfare of any children involved.

Key Considerations

  • Jurisdiction: Ensure the courts in United Kingdom have the legal authority to hear your case.
  • Asset Tracing: International divorces may complicate the tracing of overseas assets.
  • Child Relocation: Specific laws govern the movement of children out of United Kingdom without the consent of both parents.
